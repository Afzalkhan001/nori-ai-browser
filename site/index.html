The flagship

Agents that work while you don't

Not one-off answers — standing goals Nori pursues on its own, on a schedule, using the real browser. The only local, private, no-subscription agent platform in a browser.

Autonomous Agents

Describe a goal in plain English. Nori plans it, does it, and reports back — running in the background, logged in wherever you are.

  • Runs on a schedule or on demand
  • Clicks, fills, and submits like a person
  • Holds anything risky for your approval
Teachable Skills

Teach a repeatable task once, in plain words with {parameters}. Run it anytime — or let an agent invoke it as a building block.

  • No scripting, no brittle recordings
  • Reusable and parameterized
  • Saved locally, editable anytime

How it works

Yours in three steps

1

Download & install

Grab the installer for Windows or macOS. It’s a real browser — no account to create.

2

Add your key

Paste your OpenAI API key once. It’s stored on your device and never leaves it.

3

Put it to work

Browse, ask, and hand off goals to agents. Nori does the rest.

Local-first by design

Your data never leaves

Your browsing memory, agents, skills, and API key live in a file on your machine. Nothing is sent anywhere except the request you make to OpenAI. No analytics. No account. No server. You own all of it.
